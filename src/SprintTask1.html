McGregor takes shot at Khabib after UFC rival’s Hall of Fame induction

Is ‘The Eagle’ ever coming back to the UFC? The question has puzzled fight fans and pundits ever since the former Lightweight legend Khabib Nurmagomedov put down his gloves. Ever since his retirement, he had been engrossed in training fighters and stepped up as a coach to Dagestani prodigies like Islam Makhachev and Usman Nurmagomedov. Now, almost 3 years after his retirement, his cousin has given rise to a fan frenzy thanks to his recent revelations about his conversation with ‘The Eagle.’ How? Well, recounting his personal experiences with the UFC Hall-of-Famer, Usman Nurmagomedov has made some intriguing remarks about the possibility of his cousin’s comeback. So, what’s he saying?
